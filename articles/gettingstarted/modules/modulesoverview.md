<h1 id="supplychain365-supplychain365-modules-overview">SupplyChain365 SupplyChain365 Modules overview</h1>

<p>Supplychain365 consists of modules. Modules are activated by enabling them under:  Admin &gt; Licenses in the SupplyChain365 portal. Each module contains specific functionalities that are important for the relevant processes.</p>
<p>Short summary of features of each module:</p>
<p>Warehouse</p>
<p>Transform your warehouse operation and eliminate paper processes. Optimize inbound, storage and outbound of goods using barcode scanning or RFID. Use integrated wireless mobile technology to collaborate in real-time between the back office and the logistics shop fl oor. Use workfl ows to automate routine tasks. Use the automated inventory to quickly count inventory with a mobile barcode scanner. Register serial numbers, lot numbers and print barcode labels. Process pallet deliveries using license plates as logistic unit.
Transport</p>
<p>Digital transformation of the transport process, delivery and collection of goods. Easily convert orders to transport orders. Assign drivers and trucks or cars and plan trips automatically or manually. Show the progress of a trip and the position of the load/driver/truck on a map in real time. Collect signatures and data from every job and trip in real time. Record the start and end time of each order and consignment. Set up a question path to collect additional data about each transport activity. Schedule vehicle docking time and use the integrated warehouse vehicle pick and load capabilities to make the transportation process work in tandem with warehouse operation.</p>
<p>Collaborate &amp; Exchange</p>
<p>This module is especially for transport and storage companies that perform logistics for third parties. 3PL. Register inbound, storage and outbound per client. Easily report real-time stock positions per client and keep track of which stock, fhas been where, for how long. Make use of effi cient cross docking and minimize waiting times for loading and unloading trucks. Set which rates are used for charging to the customer per client and per activity. Automatic reporting of custody fees per 3PL customer. Works seamlessly with the Warehouse, Transport and Collaborate &amp; Exchange modules for flawless execution of 3PL processes.</p>
<p>3PL &amp; VAL</p>
<p>This module is especially for transport and storage companies that perform logistics for third parties. 3PL. Register inbound, storage and outbound per client. Easily report real-time stock positions per client and keep track of which stock, has been where, for how long. Make use of efficient cross docking and minimize waiting times for loading and unloading trucks. Set which rates are used for charging to the customer per client and per activity. Automatic reporting of custody fees per 3PL customer. Works seamlessly with the Warehouse, Transport and Collaborate &amp; Exchange modules for flawless execution of 3PL processes.</p>
