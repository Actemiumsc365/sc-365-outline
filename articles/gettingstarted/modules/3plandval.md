<h1 id="supplychain365-supplychain365-features-3pl--value-added-logistics">SupplyChain365 SupplyChain365 Features: 3PL &amp; Value added logistics</h1>

## 3PL customer definition
Create 3PL customers and record all customer data. Use the integrated CRM system for 3PL customers.
## 3PL items
Create items for a specific 3PL customer so a 3PL partner can store items in the warehouse.
## 3PL Inbound orders
Register incoming inventory from 3PL customers using 3PL inbound orders.
## 3PL Outbound orders
Register 3PL result orders to store stock on behalf of the 3PL customer to the right client.
## 3PL integration WMS and TMS
Use the integrated modules of Warehouse &amp; Transport to perform 3PL activities and register them in the system.
## Inventory management for third parties
Keep track of which stock and items of which 3PL are customers. Use the integrated WMS third-party inventory management system.
## 3PL &amp; VAL activities
Automatically register all warehouse and transport activities per 3PL customer and link rates with scales to this.
## 3PL invoice
Automatically generate invoices for warehouse and transport activities carried out for 3PL customers. Adjust the invoice for sending and booking.
## 3PL VAL reports
Reporting to a comprehensive specification from a certain period of time to show all 3PL VAL activities.
