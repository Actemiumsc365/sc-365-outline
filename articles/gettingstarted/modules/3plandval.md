<h1 id="supplychain365-supplychain365-features-3pl--value-added-logistics">SupplyChain365 SupplyChain365 Features: 3PL &amp; Value added logistics</h1>

<p>TABLE OF CONTENTS

3PL customer definition
Create 3PL customers and record all customer data. Use the integrated CRM system for 3PL customers.</p>
<p>3PL items
Create items for a specific 3PL customer so a 3PL partner can store items in the warehouse.</p>
<p>3PL Inbound orders
Register incoming inventory from 3PL customers using 3PL inbound orders.</p>
<p>3PL Outbound orders
Register 3PL result orders to store stock on behalf of the 3PL customer to the right client.</p>
<p>3PL integration WMS and TMS
Use the integrated modules of Warehouse &amp; Transport to perform 3PL activities and register them in the system.</p>
<p>Inventory management for third parties
Keep track of which stock and items of which 3PL are customers. Use the integrated WMS third-party inventory management system.</p>
<p>3PL &amp; VAL activities
Automatically register all warehouse and transport activities per 3PL customer and link rates with scales to this.</p>
<p>3PL invoice
Automatically generate invoices for warehouse and transport activities carried out for 3PL customers. Adjust the invoice for sending and booking.</p>
<p>3PL VAL reports
Reporting to a comprehensive specification from a certain period of time to show all 3PL VAL activities.</p>
