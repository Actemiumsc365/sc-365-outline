<p>Introduction to the SupplyChain365 Portal
In the Supplychain365 portal, administrative matters can be carried out by a user with administrative rights. It provides insight into consumption and active licenses. It is also possible to manage licenses, users, mobile devices, printers, label layouts and access for external partners.</p>
<p>License management
Manage SupplyChain365 licenses. Increase or decrease the number of licenses actually needed for mobile users and modules in the SupplyChain365 admin portal on a daily basis.</p>
<p>User management
Manage SupplyChain365 portal users for management tasks (Admin) and common tasks (Users).</p>
<p>Environment management
Manage Business Central environments (Production &amp; Sandboxes) and the connection to SupplyChain365.</p>
<p>Manage mobile Android devices
Add mobile devices and connect them to SupplyChain365 for warehouse workers and drivers.</p>
<p>Manage label printers
Add label printers that are used in the organization to use in the logistics process.</p>
<p>Manage label layouts
Manage and edit printing label layouts from License Plate labels to serial number lists.</p>
<p>Manage access for partners (collaborate &amp; exchange)
Add External Users to SupplyChain365 Chain partners (customers and suppliers) and give them access to your partner portal to collaborate digitally.</p>
<p>License and services consumption
View the consumption of SupplyChain365 licenses and API services at any time and view monthly overviews.</p>
<p>Invoices &amp; payments
Insight into the invoices and payments of the current (estimate) and months in the past from the consumption of modules and licenses.</p>
