<h1 id="how-to-view-supplychain365-license-cost">How to view SupplyChain365 License Cost</h1>

<p>In the SupplyChain365 Portal</p>
<ul>
<li>click admin and then costs to navigate to Cost current month or Cost overview.</li>
<li>On the page Cost current month, all information about your monthly cost is displayed: product name, pricing name, frequency, invoice unit, cost, quantity, days and total.</li>
<li>Here you see the total expense of the current month.</li>
<li>On the page Cost overview, you will see name, month and year, and status, which shows whether the payment of a particular invoice is open, paid, approved or payment is pending.</li>
<li>The last column displays the total costs for that specific month.</li>
</ul>
