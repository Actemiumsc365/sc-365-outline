<h1 id="how-to-get-the-supplychain365-extension">How to get the SupplyChain365 extension</h1>

<p>Installing the SupplyChain365 extension:</p>
<p>Go to the Microsoft Edge browser. Login to Business Central.
In Business Central click on the search icon or press Alt+Q and type: 'extension marketplace'.
The AppSource page will be opened with a list of all available apps for Business Central.
Alternatively, you can go to the AppSource website and install the Extension from there.
In the search bar enter: 'SupplyChain365' and press enter.
In the search results the SupplyChain365 App will be displayed.
By clicking the 'Free trial' button a confirmation window will be displayed.
Click the 'Free trial' button again to install the SupplyChain365 Extension.
SupplyChain365 has now been installed and is available for all companies within the environment.</p>
