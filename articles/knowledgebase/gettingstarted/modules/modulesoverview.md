# SupplyChain365 Modules Overview

SupplyChain365 consists of modules that can be activated by enabling them under **Admin > Licenses** in the SupplyChain365 portal. Each module contains specific functionalities that are important for the relevant processes.

---

## Available Modules

### 📦 Warehouse Module

![Warehouse Module](https://s3-eu-central-1.amazonaws.com/euc-cdn.freshdesk.com/data/helpdesk/attachments/production/103054352768/original/cRGmw3FOdvwUTf8PK0Z_TwH60k0JviJoLQ.png?1696516515)

Transform your warehouse operation and eliminate paper processes. Optimize inbound, storage and outbound of goods using barcode scanning or RFID. Use integrated wireless mobile technology to collaborate in real-time between the back office and the logistics shop floor.

**Key Features**:
- 📱 **Mobile Technology** — Real-time collaboration between back office and warehouse floor
- 🔄 **Workflow Automation** — Automate routine tasks with intelligent workflows
- 📊 **Automated Inventory** — Quick inventory counting with mobile barcode scanners
- 🏷️ **Tracking & Labels** — Register serial numbers, lot numbers, and print barcode labels
- 📦 **Pallet Management** — Process pallet deliveries using license plates as logistic units
- 📡 **RFID Support** — Advanced tracking with RFID technology

---

### 🚚 Transport Module

![Transport Module](https://s3-eu-central-1.amazonaws.com/euc-cdn.freshdesk.com/data/helpdesk/attachments/production/103054352885/original/vv6_sKFwEiE1tmqKJkfUcaQMeYpJjvzzeQ.png?1696516529)

Digital transformation of the transport process, delivery and collection of goods. Easily convert orders to transport orders with comprehensive planning and tracking capabilities.

**Key Features**:
- 🗺️ **Real-Time Tracking** — Monitor trip progress and driver/vehicle position on live maps
- 📋 **Assignment Management** — Assign drivers and trucks, plan trips automatically or manually
- ✍️ **Digital Documentation** — Collect signatures and data from every job and trip in real-time
- ⏱️ **Time Recording** — Track start and end time of each order and consignment
- 📝 **Custom Data Collection** — Set up question paths to collect additional transport activity data
- 🚪 **Dock Scheduling** — Schedule vehicle docking times with integrated warehouse pick and load capabilities
- 🔗 **Warehouse Integration** — Seamless coordination between transportation and warehouse operations

---

### 🤝 Collaborate & Exchange Module

![Collaborate & Exchange Module](https://s3-eu-central-1.amazonaws.com/euc-cdn.freshdesk.com/data/helpdesk/attachments/production/103054353035/original/t6KBUnWZN-uz77iPR34nOnMLsZ9jYh2YtA.png?1696516547)

Specialized module for companies that need to collaborate and exchange data with external partners in their supply chain ecosystem.

**Key Features**:
- 🔄 **Data Exchange** — Seamless integration with chain partners
- 📡 **EDI Support** — Electronic Data Interchange capabilities
- 🤝 **Partner Collaboration** — Real-time information sharing with suppliers and customers
- 📊 **Visibility** — Complete supply chain transparency across organizations

---

### 🏢 3PL & VAL Module

![3PL & VAL Module](https://s3-eu-central-1.amazonaws.com/euc-cdn.freshdesk.com/data/helpdesk/attachments/production/103054353114/original/VLgETT_5ANmLIbjE6v2KgBxpSwAdwnIdLA.png?1696516560)

This module is especially designed for transport and storage companies that perform logistics for third parties (3PL - Third Party Logistics).

**Key Features**:
- 👥 **Multi-Client Management** — Register inbound, storage, and outbound per client
- 📊 **Real-Time Stock Positions** — Easily report stock positions per client
- 📍 **Stock Tracking** — Keep track of which stock has been where, for how long
- 🚛 **Cross Docking** — Efficient cross-docking to minimize waiting times for loading/unloading
- 💰 **Rate Management** — Set charging rates per client and per activity
- 📈 **Automatic Reporting** — Automated custody fee reporting per 3PL customer
- 🔗 **Module Integration** — Works seamlessly with Warehouse, Transport, and Collaborate & Exchange modules

**Benefits**:
- Complete 3PL process execution
- Transparent billing per client
- Efficient operations for multiple customers
- Real-time visibility for all stakeholders

---

## Module Activation

To activate modules in your SupplyChain365 environment:

1. Navigate to **Admin > Licenses** in the SupplyChain365 portal
2. Select the modules you need for your operations
3. Enable the selected modules
4. Configure module-specific settings as required

---

## Module Integration

All SupplyChain365 modules are designed to work seamlessly together, providing a comprehensive supply chain execution platform:

- **Warehouse + Transport** — Complete logistics operation from storage to delivery
- **3PL & VAL + Warehouse + Transport** — Full 3PL service capability
- **Collaborate & Exchange + All Modules** — Extended ecosystem collaboration
