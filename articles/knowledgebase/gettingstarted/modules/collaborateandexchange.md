<h1 id="supplychain365-supplychain365-features-collaborate--exchange">SupplyChain365 SupplyChain365 Features: Collaborate & Exchange</h1>

## Partner portal login

Via the portal it is possible to invite partners to provide insight into the administration. For example, a supplier can view purchase orders and warehouse receipts. A customer can view the sales orders and warehouse shipments. Finally, a 3PL client can view the 3PL incoming and outgoing orders, warehouse receipts and shipments and an up-to-date insight into warehouse stock per location.

## Company details

Through the company details you can view the details of the company and its contacts.

## Documents customer

A customer can view the sales orders and warehouse shipments. Views can be saved and exported to Excel.

## Documents vendor

A supplier can view purchase orders and warehouse receipts. Views can be saved and exported to Excel.

## Documents 3PL customer

A 3PL client can view the 3PL incoming and outgoing orders, warehouse receipts and shipments and an up-to-date insight into warehouse stock.

## Stock information

A 3PL client can view de actual stock in the warehouse, the different locations that are available and the license plates that are used. Views are made and saved by the client and exports are possible to Excel.

## EDI Wizard profile

Within the wizard profile the partner can choose the way how files are exchanged (by services like OneDrive and Storage Accounts).

## EDI Wizard template

Within the template wizard it is possible to map the fields from EDI to business central fields of the documents that are used in the process.
