# Create items in the partner portal

**Create items in the partner portal(3PL)**

Open the partner portal under company selection you see different links connected with different customers or vendors.

Select a link that connected with a **3PL customer**.

Then click on the partner button and select an item to see the item list page. Here you can see all the items and you can add new items.

Click on the ***plus or new button*** to add a new item:

* *Vendor item number*
* *Variant code*
* *Description*
* *Unite of measure*
* *Quantity per unit of measure*
* *Gross weight*
* *Net weight*
* *Unit volume*
* *Serial item:**if the item have serial number.**(for item tracking)*
* *Lot item:* if the item has lot number. (for item tracking)

When the form is complete click on save to add new item.

Here you see the newly added item.
