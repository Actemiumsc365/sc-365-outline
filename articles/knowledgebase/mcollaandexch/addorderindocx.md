# How to add a order by use the default Excel template

**Add an order by using Excel template**

Open the partner portal under company selection you see different links connected with different customers or vendors.

Select a link that connected with a customer.

Then click on documents and select orders to see the orders page.

Click on the Uploads button and choose to download the template.

this is an interesting feature for someone who is not good at typing or likes to work on filling the paper.

For example:

* ***Print this simple Excel template:***which is available for download. Fill out the form and complete order details then transfer to a computerized version and upload it to partner portal.
* ***Download and send the template:*** it to your employee to complete the order then upload it to the partner portal simple and easy, and the order is created.

here you can download a simple template then fill in the template and upload it.

Okay, now I am going to edit the downloaded template and upload it to the partner portal.

You have seen that I have downloaded the simple template, opened it, edited it, and uploaded it back to the partner portal, here is our uploaded file and this is its number here the status shows that the document is sent or is in sending. After upload completes.

please refresh to see the latest status of the file.

Here you see that we have uploaded the template and it was sent. We send the sales order from a template.
