# Options for a customer in the partner portal

**Options for a customer in the partner portal**  
First, you must give your partner access by sending them an email with an invitation link then your partner needs to accept the invitation through the link in their inbox or junk box and start the process of creating an account.

After accepting the invitation and registering to the partner portal you have an insight into all partners and which of them accepted the invitation or are still not accepted.

**There are three different types of Partners:**

we distinguish each partner from other partners in which they get access to specific parts of the warehouse Administration.

Here are the options for a customer, a customer can see all the company details like,

**Purchase Orders:** A purchase order is a document issued by a buyer to a seller indicating the goods or services they wish to purchase, the quantity, price, and delivery details.

It is a binding agreement between the buyer and the seller, and it is used to request payment for the goods or services being purchased. Purchase orders are typically used by businesses when placing orders with suppliers or vendors.

***in purchase orders***, a customer can create a new purchase order, upload a template or repeat an order to create the same order once again,  
**Warehouse Receipt:**A warehouse receipt is a document that is issued by a warehouse or distribution center to acknowledge the receipt of goods from a supplier.

It includes information such as the date of receipt, the quantity and description of the goods received, and the location of the goods within the warehouse.

Warehouse receipts are used to track inventory and to verify that goods have been delivered in good condition,

***in Warehouse receipt***, you see all warehouse receipts and see the information about whether it is posted or not posted.  
**Purchase Return Orders:**A purchase return order is a document issued by a buyer to a seller to request the return of goods that were previously purchased.  
The document includes information such as the reason for the return, the quantity and description of the goods being returned, and any applicable return instructions.

Purchase return orders are used to manage returns and to ensure that the goods are properly credited back to the buyerâ€™s account.

***in purchase return orders***, you see all purchase return orders and see the information about whether it is posted or not.  
**Purchase Invoices:**A purchase invoice is a document issued by a seller to a buyer to request payment for goods or services that have been delivered or provided.  
It includes information such as the date of delivery or service, the quantity and description of the goods or services provided, and the amount owed by the buyer.  
Purchase invoices are used to track payments and to ensure that the buyer has paid for the goods or services received.  
**in purchase invoices,**you see all purchase invoices and see whether it is posted or not.

**Purchase Credit Memos:**A purchase credit memo is a document issued by a seller to a buyer to credit back the amount owed for goods or services that were previously purchased.  
It includes information such as the reason for the credit, the amount being credited, and any applicable return instructions.

Purchase credit memos are used to correct errors or to provide refunds to buyers.

in purchase credit memos, you see all credit memos and see the information about whether it is posted or not.
