# Create addresses in the partner portal

**Create addresses in the partner portal**

Open the partner portal under company selection you see different links connected with different customers or vendors.

Select a link that connected with a **3PL customer**.

Then click on the partner button and select addresses to see the address list page. Here you can see all addresses and you can add a new address.

Click on the **plus or new button** to add a new address:

* ***Partner name***
* ***GLN code***
* ***Address***
* ***Address 2***
* ***Postcode***
* ***City***
* ***Country code***
* ***contact***

When the form is complete click on save to add new address. Here you see the newly added address.
