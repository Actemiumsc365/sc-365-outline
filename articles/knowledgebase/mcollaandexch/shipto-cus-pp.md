# How to see shipments to send items to a customers of a partner (only for 3PL customer, customer)

**How to see shipments to send items to a customer of a partner**

**(*Only for 3PL customers, vendor*)**

Open the partner portal under company selection you see different links connected with different customers or vendors.

Select a link that connected with a vendor or a 3PL customer.

Then click on documents and select shipment to see all orders like new orders, closed orders, or upload an order from an Excel document.

**Document:** In the document page choose the document tab and click on the plus button or new,

Complete the form enter the order date and reference then complete the item form select the item unit of measure quantity, etc. then click save,

By clicking on the new button, you can add a new item to the current order and add as many items as you want then if you donâ€™t want any of the items or you selected them by mistake just click delete. after completion click ok to create a shipment order.

**Closed:** here you see a closed shipment document with all details about the document like, number, order date location code, line count, and total quantity.

**Uploads:**here you can download a simple template then fill in the template and upload it. this is an interesting feature for someone who is not good at typing or likes to work on filling the paper. For example

* **Print this simple Excel template:**which is available for download from here, just click download template. Fill out the form and complete order details then transfer to a computerized version and upload it to the partner portal.
* **Download the Excel document template:**and send it to your employee to complete the order then upload it to the partner portal simple and easily, the order is created.
