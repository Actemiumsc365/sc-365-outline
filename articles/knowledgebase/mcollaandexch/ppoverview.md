**how to work with Partners in the SupplyChain365 system.**

The partner portal of SupplyChain365 provides customers, suppliers(vendors), and 3PL customers visibility.

into the status of orders and inventory positions, let clients and customers enter or upload their orders through your SupplyChain365 portal.

Therefore, you need a license for the module collaborate and exchange, and a license for each user you want to add.

With the module collaboration and exchange it is possible to work digitally with your supplychain365 partners.

This is how to log in to the Supplychain365 partner portal.

First, open the partner portal, the link provided by the inviting party.

Then click login to enter the main page of the SupplyChain365 partner portal.  
Note: The SupplyChain365 Partner Portal works with Microsoft and the company’s registered email address.  
Here we see several different menus, so let’s look at them one by one.  
 On the right side, there is the Supplychain365 logo. By clicking it you see current user information like email, item and email, company details and sign-out button. Next to the SupplyChain365 logo, you see the language menu where you can choose between different languages.

With the buttons on the left side, you can navigate to all the important pages, such as:

* Company selection page that displays which company is currently using the SupplyChain365 Partner Portal. Here Sky Net inter drones have been selected.

**Under the company selection we have different entities:**

1. Sky net Cronus nl tutorial company item is drones and diana ross is a customer.
2. Sky net Cronus nl tutorial company item is interdrons houston is 3PL customer.
3. Sky net Cronus nl tutorial company vendor is windvanger BV mercury (vendor)

* ***Partner:*** in this section, we have company details for the customer and vendor, and for 3PL customer, we have addresses and items with company details. (Click here for more details)
* ***Documents:***show which type of document the customer, 3PL customer, or vendor has access to it. Like: orders, receipts, invoices, etc. (for more details click here)
* ***Exchange profile or EDI:***show which type of document can the customers and vendor exchange between supplychian365 and the partner portal.
* ***Stock:***This button is available just for 3Pl customers and you’re not able to see this option on customer and vendor. Here you see information about items, license plates, and locations where the items are stored (for more information click here)

This is a general overview of the SupplyChain365 partner portal you can see more information and video tutorials about each of these sections by complete details in upcoming videos. Stay tuned with supplychian365.
