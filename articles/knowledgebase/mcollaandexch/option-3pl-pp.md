**Stock:** Stock items are products or goods that are held in inventory for sale or distribution.

These items are typically stored in warehouses or storage facilities until they are needed for orders or shipments.

**Items:** Managing stock items involves tracking quantities, organizing storage locations, and optimizing inventory levels to meet demand while minimizing carrying costs.

**License plates:** is unique identifiers assigned to individual items or pallets within a warehouse.

These identifiers help in tracking and locating specific stock items within the facility.

They can be used in conjunction with barcode scanning or RFID technology to streamline inventory management and improve accuracy in picking and packing processes.

****Locations:**** are physical areas within a warehouse or distribution center where stock items are stored.

Efficient organization of locations is essential for maximizing storage space utilization and facilitating smooth inbound and outbound operations.
