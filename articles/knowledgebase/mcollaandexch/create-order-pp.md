# Create an order manual in the partner portal

**Create an order manual in the partner portal!**

1. Open the partner portal under company selection you see different links connected with different customers or vendors.
2. Select a link that connected with a **customer**.
3. Then click on documents and select orders to see the orders page.
4. In the orders page choose the document tab and click on the **plus button or new**,
5. Complete the form enter the order date and reference then complete the item form, select item, variant, unite of measure, quantity, and click save,
6. By clicking on the **new button**, you can add a new item to the current order add as many items as you want
7. And click delete if you don’t want any of the items or they entered it by mistake, so you can click delete to remove the unwanted items. Also don’t forget to complete the address information after completion click ok to create order.
