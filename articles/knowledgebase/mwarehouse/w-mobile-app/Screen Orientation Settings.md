How to change screen orientation?

click on the hamburger menu and select the screen orientation setting.

On the screen orientation setting page, we have three options

1. **Landscape:**by clicking the landscape button, the screen orientation will change and from now on you will be using the supplychain365 application in landscape mode.
2. **Portrait:** by clicking the portrait button, the screen orientation will change and from now on you will be using the supplychain365 application in portrait mode.
3. **Free turn:**by clicking this option your device will be adjusted automatically, for example, if you change the device direction the application automatically changes its direction to landscape or portrait as your device.

**Note:**by default, it has been configured as your device’s default setting.
