# How to Execute Movements from the Mobile APP

**Movement**

Goods Movements within the Warehouse. For every goods movement in **(WMS)**or warehouse management system, you move materials from one storage type to another based on a transfer order. You record all material information that is relevant for goods movements in the transfer order. Log in to the SupplyChain365 mobile application and click on move process.

**Execute movement created in Business Central:**

* Click on button move. Here you see if we have movements registered in business central.
* Click on item. This is take screen,
* Select or scan bin. In here, you see go to bin you can select different bin also in the middle of screen we have information about item, its variant, quantity and movement number. click next.
* Now take item, scan or select item click next
* Movement quantity, select how much would you like to move, then click move. This is Place screen,
* Select or scan bin, in place screen scan the bin
* place the item, scan or select the item to be placed and click next,
* Movement quantity, select how much would you like to place, than click place.
* this is how to use mobile application for movement execution in business central.

**Create a new movement directly by the mobile application:**

* Click new to register new movement via mobile application, here are the steps. Click new button,
* This is take screen, create new movement order,
* Select or scan a bin code where you want to take item,
* Select item and enter quantity to move than click on move,
* Click on place to see place screen,
* This is place screen.
* Select or scan bin which you want to place the item,
* Select item or scan item number or identifier code, click next.
* Select quantity to place and click place button.
* New movement executed.

**Note:** to see the movement you need to login to business central and see all registered and other movement documents.
