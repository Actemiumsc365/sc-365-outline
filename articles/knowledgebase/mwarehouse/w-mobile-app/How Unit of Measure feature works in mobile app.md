**What is units of measure.**  
The Unit of Measure (**UOM**): is the unit in which the quantity of an item is managed. For example, a product may be purchased in cases, stocked in boxes, and issued in single units. Various **UOMs** may exist for a single item.  
The term “*unit of measure*” can also refer to the way in which an item is measured. For example, an item may be measured by weight, by volume, or by length. When used in this context, the term “UOM” stands for “Unit of Measurement”.  
click on hamburger menu and select, Units of measure.

with this option you can scan an item number or item identifier code to see information about its measurements, for example: number, description, unit of measure in box, in piece or in carton, quantity, length, width, height and weight.

Click on item to update its unit of measure.  
Quantity is 1 in default, code is not changeable, enter 44 centimetre in length, enter 33 in width, enter 22 in height, select centimetre as volume unit of measure, enter 5 in weight and select kilogram as unit of measure. This is new adjustments click confirm to apply the changes.

**swipe right** click on triangle to see item details, like, the item's measurements, for example: number, description, unit of measure in box, in piece or in carton, quantity, length, width, height and weight and a photo of item.  
at the bottom of screen you see print button, click on print, change printer setting regarding item. and print again.
