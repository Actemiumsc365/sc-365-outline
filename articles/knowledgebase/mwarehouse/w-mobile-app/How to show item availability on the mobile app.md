# How to show item availability on the mobile app

**Introduction to Supply Chain 3 65 mobile application Hamburger Menu**  
In this video,  
your able to do the following task.  
**how to show item availability.**  
Click on hamburger menu and select, Item availability.

this option allows you to find out how many items are available in the various bins or locations.

Here I scan The Hummingbird with item number A00881 and identifier 12345006; Unit of measure: piece (stuks); and no variant.

You can scan an item number or item identifier code in order to see information about the item's measurements, for example: number, description, unit of measure in box, in piece or in carton, quantity, length, width, height and weight.  
on the right side shows total items, available items and the measurement of an item which is in pieces.  
also some item has L P number, which it means License plate number with the license plate number you will be able to track item.  
**swipe right** click on triangle to see item details, like, the item's measurements, for example: number, description, unit of measure in box, in piece or in carton, quantity, length, width, height and weight and a photo of item.  
at the bottom of screen you see print button, click on print, change printer setting regarding item. and print again.
