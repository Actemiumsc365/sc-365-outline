# Block or unblock Items in different processes.

**Block or unblock Items in different processes**

In Supplychain365 we have different types of items and by this tutorial, you can block or unblock them from different processes or because of their availability for example block an item because itâ€™s no longer available or in replenishment block for purchasing or in sales block it from sales.

**Let's start working with blocking or unblocking the items**

To block an item because of its unavailability, follow these steps:

1. Open Business Central and click on the search button then type items
2. Click on the related link to open the items page.
3. Here you can see all items and by the blocked column you can see which items are blocked so let's block an item and see its effect.
4. click on an item then on the item fast tab toggle on the blocked button then close the page
5. Now the item is blocked from all processes as you see on the item page, and it is no longer available for buy and sell.

Follow the tutorial and continue the above steps to unblock the item by toggling off the blocked button.

Let's check in Business Central that the items are available for sale or purchase or not.

**Let's block an item from the sales process.**

To block an item from selling, follow these steps:

1. Open Business Central and click on the search button then type items
2. Click on the related link to open the items page.
3. click on an item then on the prices and sales fast tab toggle on the sales blocked button then close the page
4. Now the item is blocked from sales order and it is no longer available for sale.

Follow the tutorial and continue the above steps to unblock the item by toggling off the blocked button.

Let's check in Business Central that the item is no longer available for sale.

Open the sales order complete the form and try to select the blocked item so as you see the item is no longer available.

**Let's block an item from the purchase process.**

To block an item from Purchasing, follow these steps:

1. Open Business Central and click on the search button then type items
2. Click on the related link to open the items page.
3. click on an item then on the Replenishment fast tab toggle on the purchasing blocked button then close the page
4. Now the item is blocked from the Purchase order, and it is no longer available for purchase.

Follow the tutorial and continue the above steps to unblock the item by toggling off the blocked button.

Let's check in Business Central that the item is no longer available for purchase.

Open the purchase order complete the form and try to select the blocked item so as you see the item is no longer available.
