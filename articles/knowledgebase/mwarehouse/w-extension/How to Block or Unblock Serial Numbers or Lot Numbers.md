# How to Block or Unblock Serial Numbers or Lot Numbers

**How to block or unblock serial numbers or lot numbers**

These options allow you to define whether a Serial Number or lot number is blocked or not. The default value can be set unblocked, in the field "Block ".

Blocking is used In the warehouse Defective Material is attached with serial or lot Numbers and should be blocked before sending to the Stockyard OR Unrestricted Use Stock.

**How to block a serial number**

1. Open Business Central search for ‘serial number information list’ and click the related link.
2. Once the serial number info page appears you can see which serial number is blocked in the blocked field or column.
3. Let's assume this serial number is to be blocked, click on the serial number to open the specific serial number page and then on the general fast-tab toggle on the blocked option.
4. Go back to the previous page and you will find out that the serial number is blocked.
5. To unblock the serial number, toggle off the blocked option on the serial number page.

*Note:* once the serial number is blocked you are no longer assigned to any item.

**How to block a Lot number**

1. Open Business Central search for ‘lot number information list’ and click the related link.
2. Once the lot number info page appears you can see which lot number or numbers are blocked in the blocked field or column.
3. Let's assume this Lot number is to be blocked, click on the lot number to open the specific lot number page and then on the general fast-tab toggle on the blocked option.
4. Go back to the previous page and you will find out that the Lot number is blocked.
5. To unblock the Lot number, toggle off the blocked option on the serial number page.

*Note:* once the Lot number is blocked you are no longer assigned to any item.
